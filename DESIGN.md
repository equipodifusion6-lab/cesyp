# DESIGN.md

# Government Editorial Platform Design System

## Design Vision

Create a modern institutional platform inspired by the structural strengths of large public organizations.

The platform must feel:

* trustworthy
* transparent
* organized
* active
* public-service oriented
* modern
* territorial
* accessible

The visual perception should combine:

Government Platform
+
Institutional Portal
+
Digital Newspaper
+
Public Information Hub

The experience should communicate:

"Everything happening in the territory can be understood from here."

---

# CORE DESIGN PRINCIPLES

## Information First

Content is more important than decoration.

Every design decision must improve:

* discoverability
* readability
* understanding
* navigation

---

## Territorial Awareness

The user should immediately understand:

* where things are happening
* what is happening
* who is involved
* what impact exists

---

## Trust Through Clarity

Trust comes from:

* structure
* hierarchy
* consistency
* transparency

Not from visual effects.
